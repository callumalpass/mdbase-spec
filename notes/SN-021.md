**Sections:** §9.1
**Ambiguity:** When `default_validation: "warn"`, should validation issues be returned as `warnings` alongside a successful operation result, or should the validation result itself say `valid: false` while the operation still succeeds? The test assumes the operation succeeds and warnings are reported separately.
**Assumption:** At `warn` level, the operation (create/update) succeeds and returns the result. Validation issues are reported as `warnings` in the response, not as errors. The operation is not aborted.
**Resolution:** §9.1 now clarifies that `warn` level operations succeed and `valid` remains true while issues are reported.
