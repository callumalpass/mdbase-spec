**Sections:** §3.9, §12.8
**Ambiguity:** The spec says to preserve field order, quoting style, multiline style, and comments, but does not define what constitutes "preserved" across YAML parsers that normalize formatting. It also does not say whether preserving applies to every operation or only when rewriting existing fields.
**Assumption:** Preservation applies when writing a file that already existed, and only for content not explicitly modified by the operation.
**Proposed resolution:** Add a normative rule: "When updating a file, formatting preservation applies only to untouched fields; fields explicitly modified may be re-serialized using implementation defaults."
**Resolution:** §3.5 now limits preservation to untouched fields; modified fields may be re-serialized.
