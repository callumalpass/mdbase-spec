**Sections:** §11.6
**Issue:** Two tests in expression-duration-gaps.yaml had incorrect expected dates:
- "chained duration additions": `2024-01-01 + '1M' + '15d'` = 2024-02-01 + 15d = **2024-02-16**, test expected 2024-02-15 (off by one).
- "duration subtraction chaining": `2024-03-15 - '1M' - '15d'` = 2024-02-15 - 15d = **2024-01-31**, test expected 2024-01-29 (off by two).
**Resolution:** Fixed expected dates to 2024-02-16 and 2024-01-31 respectively.
