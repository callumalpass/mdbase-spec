**Sections:** §14.1, various test files
**Severity:** High (test suite integrity)
**Issue:** Five test files are assigned to conformance levels that don't match their spec references:

| Test File | Declared Level | Spec References | Correct Level | Reason |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|---------------|--------|
| `tests/level-1/create-match-rules.yaml` | 1 | §12.1, §6.3, §6.4 | 2 | §6.3/§6.4 (match rules) are Level 2 capabilities |
| `tests/level-1/collection-layout.yaml` (group 9) | 1 | §2 | 6 | §14.1 lists "Nested collection detection" under Level 6 |
| `tests/level-1/batch-delete-gaps.yaml` | 1 | §12.4, §12.7 | 6 | §12.7 (batch operations) and §14.1 lists batch under Level 6 |
| `tests/level-1/batch-result-details.yaml` | 1 | §12.7, §2.2, §13.4 | 6 | §12.7 (batch operations) and §13.4 (cache location) are Level 6 |
| `tests/level-2/update-type-matching.yaml` | 2 | §6.3, §6.4, §12.3, §7.15 | 2 | Correct level, but ambiguous — update + path-based matching may need clarification |

The first four mean Level 1 implementations are required to pass tests for Level 2 or Level 6 features, which defeats the purpose of tiered conformance. The `create-match-rules.yaml` issue is related to SN-066: the match rule check was added to §12.1 (Level 1 create) but references Level 2 match rule evaluation.
**Proposed resolution:**
1. Move `create-match-rules.yaml` to `tests/level-2/` or add a conformance note to §12.1 step 6 that says "This step applies only when the implementation supports match rules (Level 2+). Level 1 implementations that do not evaluate match rules MAY skip this check."
2. Extract group 9 from `collection-layout.yaml` into a separate `tests/level-6/nested-collection.yaml`.
3. Move `batch-delete-gaps.yaml` to `tests/level-6/`.
4. Move `batch-result-details.yaml` to `tests/level-6/`.
**Resolution:** Moved `create-match-rules.yaml` to Level 2; moved nested collection tests to `tests/level-6/nested-collections.yaml`; moved batch tests to Level 6.
