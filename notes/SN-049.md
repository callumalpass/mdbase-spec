**Sections:** §11.5
**Issue:** The spec defined `.matches(regex)` but did not specify case sensitivity, anchoring behavior, or whether flags could be passed. Different implementations could disagree on whether `.matches("test")` is case-sensitive and whether it requires a full-string match or partial match.
**Resolution:** §11.5 updated with `.matches()` semantics paragraph: case-sensitive by default, non-anchored (partial match), use `^...$` anchors for full-string match. Implementations MAY support regex literal syntax with flags.
