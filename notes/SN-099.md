**Sections:** §2.8, §13.9, §14.8
**Issue:** Small clarity improvements.

- **§2.8**: `"This behavior ensures that collections remain self-contained and do not interfere with each other."` — Vague. More precise: `"ensuring the parent collection's scan cannot unintentionally include a sub-project's records."`
- **§13.9**: The Pros/Cons subheadings under each cache backend option are informal compared to the rest of the spec's tone. Consider a small comparison table or plain prose.
- **§14.8**: The closing bullet `"Useful for real-world applications"` is a low bar. Consider adding: `"Testable: every MUST can be verified by a conformance test."`
