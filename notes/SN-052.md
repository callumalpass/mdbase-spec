**Sections:** §10.7
**Issue:** §10.7 stated "Ungrouped results (null/missing group value) appear in a separate group" but did not specify where in the group ordering the null group appears. This matters for deterministic output.
**Resolution:** §10.7 updated: the null group sorts LAST in ASC direction and FIRST in DESC direction, consistent with the `order_by` null handling rules in §10.3.
