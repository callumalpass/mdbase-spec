**Sections:** §1 (Terminology), §10.5, §14.1
**Ambiguity:** Terminology says body queries are optional, but §10.5 defines `file.body` rules with SHOULDs and Level 3 includes `body_search` as required category. This creates a contradiction: body queries appear optional in the glossary but mandatory for Level 3 conformance.
**Assumption:** Body query support is REQUIRED at Level 3 (as per §14.1), and the terminology line should be treated as a general statement about implementations not claiming Level 3.
**Proposed resolution:** Clarify §1 to say "body queries are optional unless implementing Level 3+," or move the optional statement into a non-normative note.
**Resolution:** §1 now states body querying is optional unless Level 3+ is claimed.
