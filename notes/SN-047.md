**Sections:** §3
**Issue:** yaml-multiline-gaps.yaml "folded block with keep and indentation (>+2)" expected `"  Folded with spaces and trailing newlines.\n\n"` (lines folded). Same issue as SN-045: with `>+2` (base indent 2) and content at 4 spaces, the lines are "more-indented" per YAML spec §8.2.1, so newlines are preserved. The `+` chomping indicator keeps trailing newlines but does not affect the more-indented behavior. All YAML parsers agree.
**Resolution:** Fixed expected value to `"  Folded with spaces\n  and trailing newlines.\n\n"`.
