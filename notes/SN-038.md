**Sections:** §11.5
**Issue:** The `string.split with multi-character separator` test in expressions.yaml used separator `"::"` and expected both items/a.md and items/b.md to match. However, items/a.md has `csv: "a::b::c"` (contains `::`) while items/b.md has `csv: "x,y,z"` (uses commas, not `::`). Only items/a.md should match.
**Resolution:** Fixed `total_count` from 2 to 1 and removed items/b.md from expected results.
