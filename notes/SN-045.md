**Sections:** §3
**Issue:** yaml-multiline-gaps.yaml "folded block with explicit indentation indicator (>2)" expected the content `"  Indented folded text here.\n"` (lines folded into one). However, per the YAML 1.2 specification, content lines in a folded block (`>`) that are indented beyond the block's base indentation level are "more-indented" and their newlines are preserved (not folded). With `>2` (base indent = 2 spaces) and content at 4 spaces, the content has 2 extra spaces of indentation, making it more-indented. All YAML parsers (js-yaml, Python yaml, yaml npm) agree on this behavior.
**Resolution:** Fixed expected value to `"  Indented folded\n  text here.\n"` (newlines preserved for more-indented lines).
